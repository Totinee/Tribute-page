Dr. Hermann Gmeiner

About Dr. Hermann Gmeiner

  • Born in Alberschwende, Austria in 1919
  • Was an Austrian philanthropist
  • Came form a farming family in Vorarlberg
  • Experienced the sufferings of an orphan as his mother died at an early age
  • Understood great needs of orphaned children after the SSecond World War
  • Founded SOS Children's Village in 1949 in Imst, Tyrol
  • Got recognized worldwide as one of the most important and successful pioneers in family care for children in need
  • Died in Innsbruck in 1986
  • Had already chosen Helmut Kutin as his successor who continued his legacy

Quotes by Dr. Hermann Geminer

  • "Every big thing in the world only comes true, when somebody does more than he has to do"
  • "All the children of the world are our children"
